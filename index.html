STEM Surveyor — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06/22/2026

STEM Surveyor ("the extension") is an internal tool provided by Turing ("we", "us") for authorized company reviewers. This policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is used, where it is stored, and with whom it is shared. The extension is intended for internal workflow use and is not offered to the general public.

1. Scope

The extension runs only on the company questionnaire page at https://feedback.anthropic.com/surveyor/*. It is not active on any other website, and it does not read, collect, or monitor your general browsing activity or history.

2. Data we collect

  • Account information (via Google Sign-In): your name, email address, and profile picture. Sign-in is restricted to authorized company Google accounts.
  • Questionnaire answers: the responses you enter on the survey page (multiple-choice selections, free-text answers, and any justification fields), submitted for quality review when you choose to validate.
  • Activity metrics: the survey identifier, your reviewer email as a submission identifier, and time-on-task measurements (time spent and whether a session expired).

We do not collect financial, health, or location data, personal communications, or web browsing history.

3. How we use the data

  • To authenticate you and confirm you are an authorized company reviewer.
  • To send your answers to our review service for automated quality assessment and to display feedback to you before submission.
  • To record submissions and time-on-task metrics for internal quality-control purposes.

4. Where data is stored and transmitted

  • Locally: your signed-in profile and per-survey timing state are stored on your device using Chrome's local storage. This local data is cleared when you sign out or submit.
  • Transmitted over HTTPS to Google (googleapis.com) to verify your account during sign-in.
  • Transmitted over HTTPS to our review service (hosted on Google Cloud Run) to validate answers and record submissions.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties. Data is shared only with Google's authentication API (for sign-in) and with our own internal review service. We do not use your data for advertising, credit assessment, or any purpose unrelated to the extension's single purpose described above.

6. Data retention

Locally stored data is removed on sign-out or submission. Data received by our review service is retained only as long as necessary for internal quality-control purposes, in accordance with Turing's internal data retention practices.

7. Limited Use disclosure

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

8. Contact
